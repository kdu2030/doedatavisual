RISE

Examining the underserved counties of Virginia

What is the Rural Horseshoe?

The Rural Horshoe is made up of several counties in the state of Virginia. These counties are generally undereducated, and their residents have lower incomes compared to the rest of the state. Some of these counties include Augusta, Lee, and Wise County.

What counties are in the Rural Horseshoe?

Purpose of RISE

RISE, Rural Initiative Software Enterprise, is a web application meant to allow users to investigate and analyze the education levels and incomes of counties in the Rural Horseshoe. Using RISE, users will be able to compare Rural Horseshoe counties to the rest of Virginia.
